Automatic Power Factor Correction Panels

Automatic Power Factor Controllers

Electrical loads such as motors can cause electrical systems to be very inductive, which results in very ‘lagging power factor’ i.e., wastage of energy. The simple solution to maintain the power factor in the required range is to connect or disconnect the power factor correction capacitors. Manual switching is just impossible for rapidly fluctuating loads and hence an automatic control system is required which continuously monitors the power factor and makes appropriate corrections to maintain it within the required range.

System Design

Baron Automatic Power Factor Correction System is unique with its custom-based design, based on the load study conducted on-site with a high-speed power quality analyzer. It is designed to suppress transient inrush current and ensure the longevity of capacitors. Sequential cyclic switching and carefully selected components enhance performance and safety.

Salient Features

  • Microprocessor-based Power Factor Controller with four-quadrant measurement
  • Accuracy of ±0.8% for Reactive Power, ±0.5% for Current
  • Continuous monitoring of defective capacitor stages
  • Alarm for excess operation beyond programmed limits
  • Dual control curve characteristics for optimized performance

System Protection: Incoming molded case circuit breakers and capacitor duty contactors ensure safety during operation.

Direct Benefits

  • Reduction in MD kVA
  • Reduction in kWh Consumption
  • Reduction in kVAh Charges
  • Avoidance of PF Penalty
  • Availing PF Incentive

Indirect Benefits

  • Reduction/Avoidance of End Termination Burnouts
  • Reduction/Avoidance of Motor Winding Failures
  • Improved System Performance due to Healthy Voltage
  • Low Electric Breakdowns/Reduced Plant Downtime
